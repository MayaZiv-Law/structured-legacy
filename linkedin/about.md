# About Section — Maya Ziv

_LinkedIn limit: 2,600 chars. First ~210 chars appear above the "...see more" fold on mobile — this is the single most-read sentence on your profile. Treat it like a subject line._

_Goal: **more visibility → more inbound leads.** Hook must earn the click, keywords must earn the search rank, CTA must earn the email._

## Current (baseline — 3 sentences, ~410 chars)

> My work focuses on complex situations where law, finance and business intersect.
>
> Before entering legal practice I worked at Citi Innovation Lab and Vornado Realty Trust in roles where financial and legal decisions were intertwined. I hold a degree in Finance and Accounting from Baruch College in New York, a background that continues to shape how I approach legal risk.

**Problems:**
- No hook — first line is abstract
- "I" focus instead of "you/client" focus
- No specific practice areas, deal types, or industries → weak search match
- No social proof beyond company names
- No CTA → no way for an interested lead to reach you
- Finance background is the moat, but it reads like a footnote

## Why this structure works for lead generation

| Section | Purpose | 2026 algorithm signal |
|---|---|---|
| Hook (line 1–3) | Above-the-fold stop-scroll | Completion-reads rewarded |
| What I do (line 4) | ICP + outcome in one sentence | Keyword relevance for search |
| Proof (Citi/Vornado/Baruch) | Credibility with brand anchors | Profile completeness |
| Practice areas (bullets) | Searchable keywords | Recruiter + inbound search |
| Working-style line | Personality + fit filter | Differentiation |
| **CTA with email** | Gives leads a next step | Direct conversion (skips DMs) |

## Customization TODOs before you publish

1. Replace `[EMAIL]` with a real intake email (not LinkedIn InMail — direct email converts 3–5x higher for lead gen).
2. Confirm the website URL (or remove the line if not live yet).
3. If shareable, add **one** quantified proof point:
   - `Advised on $X+ in closed transactions`
   - `Counsel to 20+ founders across fintech, real estate, and SaaS`
4. Add a line before the CTA if you have bar admissions / speaking gigs / publications:
   > _Admitted to practice in [jurisdictions]. Speaker/writer on [topics]._
5. Emoji comfort check — `→` `•` are algorithm-safe. `📩 🌐` can be swapped for plain text if you prefer more formal.

## What I intentionally did NOT do

- **No "passionate about the law"** clichés.
- **No first-line "hi, I'm Maya"** — wastes above-the-fold real estate.
- **No multi-paragraph mission statement** — founders skim.
- **No generic "results-driven attorney"** — means nothing, costs a keyword slot.
- **No hiding the marketing/finance past** — it's the moat.
