# Headline Options — Maya Ziv

_LinkedIn limit: 220 characters. First ~60 chars visible everywhere (search, comments, connection requests). Pack keywords early._

_Goal: **more visibility → more inbound leads.** Every word must earn a keyword slot or a credibility signal._

## Current (baseline)

> Lawyer | Israel | Complex Transactions, Cross Border Matters and Risk Allocation

**Problems:**
- Pipes are dated and look generic
- "Risk Allocation" is jargon clients don't search for
- No value proposition — reads like a resume line
- Doesn't use the finance/Citi/Baruch differentiator
- Value to reader: zero

---

## Option 1 — Value-forward (recommended for lead gen)

> Helping founders & companies close complex US–Israel deals | Transactional Lawyer with a Finance background (ex-Citi Innovation Lab, Vornado) | M&A • Cross-Border • Commercial Contracts

**Length:** ~210 chars
**Why it works for leads:** leads with the outcome ("close complex deals"), names the ICP ("founders & companies") — a founder searching for counsel sees themselves in line 1. Credibility signals (Citi, Vornado) inline. Three high-intent search keywords at the end.

---

## Option 2 — Credibility-forward

> Transactional Lawyer bridging law, finance & business | Ex-Citi Innovation Lab & Vornado Realty Trust | Complex Transactions, M&A, Cross-Border (US–Israel), Commercial Agreements

**Length:** ~188 chars
**Why it works:** leads with the unique positioning, anchors with two recognizable brands, then stacks keywords. Best when your referral source is other attorneys / finance folks (pedigree matters).

---

## Option 3 — ICP-specific (personal-brand voice)

> I help founders navigate the legal-financial edge of every complex deal | US–Israel Cross-Border Counsel | M&A • Commercial Contracts • Risk | Finance-trained Lawyer (Baruch, NYC)

**Length:** ~190 chars
**Why it works:** most personal / first-person, strongest when you're running a content strategy (posts, DMs). Use if you'll commit to posting 2–3x/week.

---

## Recommendation for "more visibility → more leads"

Ship **Option 1** immediately. It wins on:

| Ranking factor | Why Option 1 wins |
|---|---|
| **LinkedIn search** | M&A + Cross-Border + US-Israel keywords in first ~110 chars |
| **Outcome clarity** | Founder scanning sees "close complex deals" in first 4 words |
| **Differentiation** | Citi + Vornado in the headline — no other Tel Aviv transactional lawyer can use them |
| **Connection-request conversion** | ICP ("founders & companies") named up front → self-selection |

If inbound picks up within 60 days and you start posting, switch to **Option 3** to match the voice of your content.
