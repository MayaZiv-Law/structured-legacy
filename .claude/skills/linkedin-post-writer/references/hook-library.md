# LinkedIn Hook Library

25 proven opening lines, grouped by hook type. The job of a hook is to earn the "...see more" click. Keep hooks under ~210 characters so the full hook appears above the fold on mobile.

Swap the bracketed slots for specifics. Specificity always outperforms generic framing.

---

## Curiosity Hooks (tease the payoff)

1. I just discovered the weirdest way to [outcome]. It has nothing to do with [expected thing].
2. There's a reason [top 1%] [do X] and no one talks about it.
3. I broke one of the biggest rules in [industry] last month. It worked better than anything I've tried in 5 years.
4. The thing no one tells you about [topic]: [setup that begs the next line].
5. I spent $[amount] learning this so you don't have to.

## Contrarian Hooks (challenge the consensus)

6. "[Common advice]" is the worst advice in [industry].
7. Unpopular opinion: [widely-held belief] is killing your [outcome].
8. Everyone's wrong about [topic]. Here's what I've actually seen.
9. Stop [doing common thing]. Start [doing this instead].
10. [Popular tool/tactic] isn't the answer. It never was.

## Stat / Data Hooks (anchor with a number)

11. [Surprising %] of [group] [do surprising thing]. I've been studying why.
12. We analyzed [N] [things]. The pattern shocked us.
13. $[big number] in [revenue/loss/pipeline] came down to one decision.
14. [N]x improvement in [metric] in [short timeframe]. Here's the exact playbook.
15. I tracked [behavior] across [N] [people/companies] for [timeframe]. The results weren't what I expected.

## Story Hooks (cinematic openings)

16. I got [bad thing] on my birthday.
17. It was [specific time] on a [day]. My phone wouldn't stop buzzing.
18. Three years ago I was [low point]. Today I [high point]. Here's what changed.
19. My [boss/mentor/client] said something in [year] that I still think about every week.
20. I almost quit [thing] last [timeframe]. Then [small moment] changed everything.

## Question Hooks (prime the reader to engage)

21. What's the most expensive mistake you've made in your career?
22. If you could only keep one skill from the last decade, which would it be?
23. When did you last change your mind about something important?
24. What's a belief you held confidently at 25 that looks ridiculous now?
25. What would you do differently if you started your [career/company/project] today?

---

## Usage notes

- **Pair hook style to format.** Story hooks belong on story posts; stat hooks anchor case studies and listicles well.
- **Test the "see more" cutoff.** Drop the hook into LinkedIn's composer or count ~210 characters. If the payoff is below the fold, tighten.
- **Avoid clickbait without delivery.** The body must honor the hook or engagement dies in the comments.
- **Rotate hook types.** If your last 3 posts all opened with contrarian takes, your audience tunes out. Mix it up.
