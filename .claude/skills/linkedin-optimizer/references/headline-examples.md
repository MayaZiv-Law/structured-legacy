# Headline Examples — Marketers

Ten vetted headlines across marketer archetypes. Each follows the formula: `[what you do] + [who you help] + [unique outcome/proof]`. All under 220 chars, 3-5 keywords each.

---

### 1. B2B SaaS — Demand Gen Lead
**Demand Generation Lead @ Acme | I help B2B SaaS companies build pipeline from paid + content | $40M+ sourced across HubSpot, Marketo, 6sense**

*Why it works:* Role keyword first ("Demand Generation Lead"), clear ICP ("B2B SaaS"), quantified proof ($40M), and three tool keywords recruiters filter on.

---

### 2. B2B SaaS — Product Marketing Manager
**Product Marketing Manager | Launching technical products to developers and PLG buyers | 4 GTM launches, 2 category-defining narratives**

*Why it works:* Names the audience (developers, PLG buyers) the way hiring managers search, and swaps vague "passionate" language for launch counts.

---

### 3. Agency — Founder
**Founder, Northbeam Collective | B2B content agency for Series A-C startups | 80+ clients, $200M+ influenced pipeline since 2021**

*Why it works:* Agency niche + stage ICP + portfolio-scale proof. "B2B content agency" is a high-intent inbound search term.

---

### 4. Freelance — Copywriter
**Freelance Conversion Copywriter | Landing pages + email sequences for SaaS & e-commerce | Avg client lift: 38% on primary CTA**

*Why it works:* "Freelance" + service keyword up front (how freelancers are sourced), specific deliverables, a benchmark outcome.

---

### 5. Founder — Bootstrapped
**Founder @ Loop Reviews | Helping DTC brands turn reviews into revenue | $8M ARR, bootstrapped, 4.9 on Shopify**

*Why it works:* Name-drops the company + category, states the business model (bootstrapped), and uses public proof (ARR + app-store rating).

---

### 6. Content Marketer — Senior IC
**Senior Content Marketer | SEO + thought leadership for fintech & dev tools | Grew organic traffic 12x at two Series B startups**

*Why it works:* Combines two high-search keywords ("SEO", "thought leadership"), names verticals, quantifies across multiple roles to show repeatability.

---

### 7. Growth Marketer — Series A/B
**Growth Marketer | Paid acquisition + lifecycle for consumer subscription apps | Scaled 3 apps past 1M MAU at <$3 CAC**

*Why it works:* Dual-discipline ("Paid acquisition + lifecycle") matches two recruiter searches at once. CAC + MAU are the exact metrics consumer hiring managers screen for.

---

### 8. Brand Marketer — Senior
**Brand Marketing Lead | Building category-defining brands in climate & hardware | Previously: Patagonia, Rivian, Allbirds**

*Why it works:* "Brand Marketing Lead" is exactly how recruiters search; logos do the proof lifting when metrics are confidential.

---

### 9. Content Marketer — Freelance Writer
**B2B Writer for SaaS & AI companies | Long-form content that ranks and converts | Bylines: First Round, Lenny's, a16z | Book via link below**

*Why it works:* Service + niche up front, outcome ("ranks and converts") covers both SEO and CRO hiring intents, byline logos signal credibility, CTA at the end.

---

### 10. Growth Marketer — Head of Growth
**Head of Growth | PLG + outbound for vertical SaaS | Took 2 companies from $1M to $10M ARR in <18 months**

*Why it works:* "Head of Growth" + "PLG" + "vertical SaaS" are three distinct recruiter filters. The ARR trajectory is the clearest possible proof of repeatability.
